How we operate

We are wiling to make any page, we do actually prefer doing custom pages if you have any interesting ideas that may give use some experience

If you want us to develope a page for you contact us in facebook, instagram, or by mail. tell us about what you what you need that page for and your ideas and we will give you a quote. the steps we take with your project.

  • You contact us and push your project and we will have or specialized developer for yor project and discus all of the details with you.

  • He will make a sketch in less than 48 h, these will be free. Here you will farther discus colors and some changes that you want to make.

  • Once you have a basics sketch you will receive a quote, and our team will start working on the page, in case you have physical objects you will need to pay for the hardware up front since we have to order it.

  • When we finish the page it will be deployed, these version will have watermarks, and you will receive an invoice. once we receive payment all the watermarks will be removed immediately and hardware will be send via mail.
